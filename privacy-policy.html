Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

This policy explains how Neon Slip handles data when you visit and play. Neon Slip is an original project by Ali Imran.

1. Basic Site Usage

Neon Slip can be played without creating an account. When you use the site, basic technical information such as browser type, device type, and approximate performance data may be processed to keep gameplay stable and improve reliability.

2. Local Storage and Cookies

The site uses browser storage and similar technologies to store settings and gameplay state, including local best score, upgrades, and preferences. If authentication or cloud features are enabled, additional identifiers may be stored for sign-in continuity.

3. Leaderboard and Account Data

If you submit scores, your display name, score, and optional profile image may appear publicly in leaderboards. If you register an account, authentication and profile data are handled by the configured backend services.

4. Analytics

Analytics tools may be added to measure gameplay usage, retention, and site performance. If enabled, analytics data is used to improve game quality and user experience.

5. Third-Party Advertising

Neon Slip may use third-party advertising providers such as Google AdSense in the future. These providers may use cookies or similar technologies to personalize ad delivery and measure campaign effectiveness according to their own policies.

6. Data Sharing

Personal data is not sold. Limited data may be processed by infrastructure vendors and service providers strictly to operate the game, authentication, leaderboard features, analytics, or advertising integrations.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: al116im24@ast.kevibham.org

8. Updates to This Policy

This page may be updated as Neon Slip evolves. Material changes should be reflected by updating the effective date.
